Our Story

A mission rooted in authentic Shotokan

Japan Karate Association Indonesia (JKA Indonesia) is the official representative of the Japan Karate Association (JKA) — one of the oldest, most prestigious Shotokan Karate organizations in the world.

Established to preserve and promote authentic Shotokan Karate, we are committed to upholding the spirit of “Oss”, discipline, and the timeless values of Budo.

Our journey in Indonesia began with a mission: to build a strong, disciplined, and respectful karate community that connects local karateka with the global JKA family. Under the guidance of certified instructors, we provide standardized training, grading, and opportunities to compete internationally.

Core Values

Preserving the True Spirit of Shotokan Karate

Authentic Shotokan Training

Structured classes focusing on Kihon (Basics), Kata (Forms), and Kumite (Sparring), all following the official curriculum set by JKA Headquarters in Japan — ensuring every movement, technique, and mindset aligns with the true spirit of Shotokan.

National & International Camps

We regularly host seminars, master camps, and invite top instructors from Japan to pass on the valuable lessons of body, mind, and spirit of true JKA Shotokan Karate.

Dan Exams & Certifications

Our members take official grading exams recognized worldwide, obtaining internationally recognized JKA certifications.

World-Class Championships

We prepare and send Indonesian karateka to compete at the Funakoshi Gichin Cup and other prestigious tournaments.
